What Nobody Told You About Andre The Giant

Early Life

Andre René Roussimoff was born in Grenoble, France, in 1946. Due to a medical condition called acromegaly, he experienced rapid growth, eventually reaching a height of 7'4" and a weight of 520 pounds. His size became legendary, making him one of the most recognizable figures in professional wrestling.

Famous Moments

Andre's wrestling career included iconic matches, most famously his showdown with Hulk Hogan at WrestleMania III in 1987. His incredible charisma and unmatched presence made him a beloved figure in sports entertainment.

Legacy

Beyond wrestling, Andre became a cultural icon. From his role in *The Princess Bride* to countless documentaries about his life, his legacy continues to inspire fans around the world.
